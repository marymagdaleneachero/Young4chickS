About Young4ChickS

Young4ChickS is an innovative urban brooder program founded by a young Member of Parliament with a vision to empower youth between 20-30 years old. Our mission is to facilitate young urban dwellers in stimulating wealth creation and meaningful participation in the money economy through accessible poultry farming.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality chicks and comprehensive support to young entrepreneurs, enabling them to build sustainable poultry businesses and contribute to food security in their communities.

What We Offer

  • Quality Chicks: Both layers and broilers, available in local and exotic varieties
  • Affordable Pricing: Fixed price of UGx 1,650 per chick, regardless of type
  • Feed Support: 2 bags of quality feed per farmer, payable after 2 months
  • Starter Support: Up to 100 chicks for new farmers
  • Growth Support: Up to 500 chicks for returning farmers
  • Regular Access: New requests every 4 months

How It Works

1. Register

Complete your registration with recommender details and get verified.

2. Request

Submit your chick request form specifying type and quantity needed.

3. Approval

Our Brooder Manager reviews and approves your request based on stock availability.

4. Collection

Collect your approved chicks and start your poultry farming journey!
